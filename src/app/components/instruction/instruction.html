The Imitation Game

Based on Alan Turing’s 1950 concept: Can a machine mimic human interaction so perfectly that you cannot tell the difference?

01

The 2-Minute Window

You have exactly 120 seconds. Analyze word choice, emotional tone, and response timing. Is that pause a human thinking, or a bot simulating a delay?

02

The Human Nuance

Watch for imperfections. A quick "sry, running late" or a personal opinion on a local park might feel human—but it could be a calculated strategy to build fake trust.

03

The Final Verdict

After the clock stops, you must decide: Biological Human or AI Model. Your detection skills are the only thing that matters.
